Awards

To date, Russell has 4 award wins and 11 nominations to her name.

Wins

  • In 2019, she won an African-American Film Critics Association (AAFCA) We See You Award for her role in Waves.
  • In 2019, she won a Gotham Independent Film Award under the Breakthrough Actor category for her role in Waves.
  • In 2020, the actress was honored with a Hollywood Critics Association Star on the Rise Award for her role in Waves as well.
  • In 2020, she managed to win a Santa Barbara International Film Festival Virtuoso Award, also for her role in Waves.

Nominations

  • In 2019, she was nominated for an Academy of Science Fiction, Fantasy & Horror Films, USA Saturn Award under the Best Supporting Actress in Streaming Presentation category for her role in Lost in Space (2018).
  • In 2020, she received two Black Reel nominations under the Outstanding Supporting Actress, Motion Picture and Outstanding Breakthrough Performance, Female categories.
  • She received a Chicago Film Critics Association Award nomination for Most Promising Performer in 2019.
  • In 2020, she was an Independent Spirit Award nominee for Best Supporting Female.
  • She was also a Georgia Film Critics Association (GAFCA) Breakthrough Award nominee in 2020. This nomination was for her roles in Escape Room and Waves.
  • In 2020, she was nominated for two HCA Awards under the Best Supporting Actress and Breakthrough Performance - Actress categories.
  • She received an IFJA Award nomination under the Best Supporting Actress category in 2019.
  • In 2020, she was nominated for a LEJA Award under the Best Performance by an Actress in a Supporting Role category.
  • In 2019, she was nominated for a Seattle Film Critics Award under the Best Supporting Actress category.
