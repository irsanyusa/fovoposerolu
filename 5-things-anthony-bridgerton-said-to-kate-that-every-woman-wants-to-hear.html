5 things Anthony Bridgerton said to Kate that every woman wants to hear

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Friday, July 5, 2024

The second season features Anthony Bridgerton and Kate Sharma who fall in love unexpectedly and weather the storms of England's high society together. A classic love story, but still enthralling.

The popularity of this show pontificates that we are all lovers at heart.

ADVERTISEMENT

The first season had the famous, “I burn for you’’, the second season follows in its trail with awesome lines.

What are some of the things Anthony Bridgerton said to Kate that every woman would love to hear?

1. "It is because I have never met anyone like you. It is maddening. How much you consume my very being."

Every woman wants to meet a man who is consumed by the thought of her such that he has no time to think of any other woman.

2. "I know I am imperfect, but I will humble myself before you because I cannot imagine my life without you.”

ADVERTISEMENT

What woman wouldn’t love to hear her man say ‘I would humble myself before you'? especially when he makes a mistake or he doesn’t know how to give her what she desires. Anthony admits his pride and hubris but he wants to work on it for Kate.

3. "I love you. I’ve loved you from the moment we raced each other in that park. I’ve loved you at every dance, on every walk, every time we’ve been together, and every time we’ve been apart."

'I love you', how powerful is a confession of love! But not only that but talking about the first time they met and all the moments they shared because it meant something to him. The beautiful thing is he says this not to hear it back or to win her over but because he means it.

4. "You are the bane of my existence and the object of all my desires."

Now when he said this everyone woman who watched the series wanted their man to say it to her. It is the depth and poetic nature of the words for us. Once more, Anthony lets her know that she is the number one woman in his life.

ADVERTISEMENT

5. "Words of flattery are beautiful and sweet, but they are also hollow unless accompanied by action."

Anthony lets her know that talk is cheap. We all know that saying you love someone and acting out of love are two different things and Anthony is prepared to back it up with actions and he does!
