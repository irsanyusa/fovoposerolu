More photos from singer's mum's wedding

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, July 25, 2024

Due to the privacy of the wedding, there were only three pictures leaked to the public, but now, there are more.

The guest/ family that were at the wedding include Kelly Rowland, Michelle Williams,Angie Beyince, and Bianca Lawson(actress/daughter of the groom).

The bride recently granted an interview to People magazine, here is what she had to say about finding love at 61;

ADVERTISEMENT

"To walk down the aisle and see all my friends and family and to see my handsome man standin' there, and to know that at 61 you can still find love and have a magical day like that … it was really beautiful. There was a time when I doubted if I would find love again. But I was determined to be happy, and God sent somebody into my life. You can find love at any age. You just have to go for it.’

She also revealed that she had known her husband, Richard Lawson, for three decades, but that they had only reconnected a year and a half ago.

During the wedding, her two grand kids, Solange’s son Julez and Beyonce’s daughter Blue Ivy, were ring bearer and flower girl.

We wish them a wonderful life ahead.
