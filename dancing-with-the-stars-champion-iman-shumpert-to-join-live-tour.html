Dancing with the Stars Season 30 champion Iman Shumpert is officially joining the show’s live tour next year. A number of pro dancers and celebrities from the show have already been announced as making appearances, and now we can add the most recent winner to the list.

Iman Shumpert Joins ‘Dancing with the Stars’ Tour

Shumpert recently won Season 30 of Dancing with the Stars with his partner Daniella Karagach, beating runner-up JoJo Siwa. The former NBA player will appear on the show’s upcoming tour on select dates, starting with January 7 in Richmond, Virginia.

Also appearing in select cities is Season 29 winner Kaitlyn Bristowe, as well as this season’s contestants Jimmie Allen and Amanda Kloots. The January 7 show in Richmond, which kicks off the tour, will also feature Bristowe and Allen along with Shumpert.

The tour will run until March 27, with the last city being Modesto, California, with special guest Amanda Kloots. There are VIP packages available that get you better seating, photo ops with the cast, and opportunities to buy merchandise early.

Which ‘DWTS’ Pros Are Appearing on the Tour?

Several Dancing with the Stars pro dancers will be taking their moves on tour to perform the ballroom dances we all know and love from the show. The remaining 2020 tour dates were canceled due to the pandemic, and the 2021 tour never happened, so it’s been quite some time since the pros have gone on the road.

This year’s winning pro Daniella Karagach will be on hand, as well as her husband Pasha Pashkov. The show will also feature Brandon Armstrong, Alan Bersten, Artem Chigvintsev, Sasha Farber, Gleb Savchenko, Emma Slater, Britt Stewart, Sofia Ghavami, and Koko Iwasaki.

You can check out which celebrities will be appearing in which cities and buy your tickets and VIP packages on the Dancing with the Stars Live tour website.
