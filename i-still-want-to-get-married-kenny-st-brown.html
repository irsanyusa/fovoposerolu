I Still Want To Get Married Kenny St. Brown

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Tuesday, July 16, 2024

Gospel music act and former wife of Eddy Ashiedu, the one-time hip hop musician, Kenny St. Brown, who now wants to be called Kenny St. Best, has said that she still hopes to get married years after her marriage which produced a kid. This she made know recently when asked whether she still plans to get married and how life has been for her as single parent. She said; “I am a cute girl and still feel cute inside; I still think of getting married. Yes I will; most definitely.” On being a single mother, she said; “I am not a single mother; I am a single parent. A single mother gets the bill taken care of while a single parent plays the role of a father and a mother while still picking up the bills. It has been a tough job anyway. I enjoy it, as I said earlier, and it is really a lovely experience being a mother.”
