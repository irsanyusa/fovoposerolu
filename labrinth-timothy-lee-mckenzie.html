Labrinth [Timothy Lee McKenzie] (Singer-Songwriter)

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, May 23, 2024

Full Name: Timothy Lee McKenzie, known by his stage name Labrinth
Profession: Singer-Songwriter, Rapper and Record Producer

Biography: Labrinth is an English singer and rapper who started out with a career in music production before moving to pursue his own work as a recording artist. He was then best known for his collaborations with Tinie Tempah, including "Pass Out" (2010) and "Frisky" (2010).

He went on to release his own solo work, including the single "Let the Sun Shine" (2010). Subsequently he released his debut album Electronic Earth (2012), and then as part of a supergroup called LSD alongside Sia and Diplo released an album titled LSD (2019).

More recently, Labrinth composed the score for season one of the television series Euphoria. He received two Emmy nominations for this work, one of which he won with the single "All for Us" (2019). He also composed the score for season two of the show, for which he received a further two Emmy nominations.

Born: January 4, 1989
Birthplace: Hackney, London, England, United Kingdom
Age: 35 years old

Generation: Millennial
Chinese Zodiac: Dragon
Star Sign: Capricorn
