Leah Marlene Teases New Music With The Release Of The First Single From Her Upcoming Project

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, May 18, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Singing American Idol

Raymond Liu via Getty Images

Reading Time: 2 minutes

American Idol alum, Leah Marlene is causing a stir among fans with her recent Instagram post. In an exciting new post, Marlene hinted at the release of her first single from an upcoming project, titled Flashes. This single is set to release on Friday, October 20.

Leah Marlene Teases Latest Musical Project With New Single

The 21-year-old singer-songwriter hailing from Normal, Illinois, took to Instagram on October 17, to share a cryptic message that has set her fans’ hearts aflutter. The post, accompanied by an enigmatic countdown clock, carried the message

“The first single off my next project, ‘Flashes,’ will be making its way to your ears this Friday.” Marlene wrote.

Ever since the release of her debut EP, Flowers, in May 2022, Marlene’s followers have been eagerly awaiting fresh tunes from this rising star. Known for her distinctive blend of pop, rock, and folk, Marlene has won hearts with her unique voice and knack for crafting both catchy and meaningful songs.

Fans were quick to notice that Marlene used hashtags that hint at the diverse musical influences driving her new project. Among them are #existentialism, #eclecticmusic, #folkmusic, #rockmusic, #alternativemusic, and references to artists like Madison Cunningham, Jade Bird, Sharon Van Etten, Margaret Glaspy, S.G. Goodman, and Lissie.

While the full details of Marlene’s upcoming project remain shrouded in mystery, her Instagram teaser hints at a thrilling musical journey that’s bound to captivate fans, both old and new. With her unique voice and her talent for crafting meaningful songs, it’s safe to say that we’re in for a musical treat.

Fans Show Outpouring Support on Her Post

The outpouring of excitement and support in Marlene’s Instagram post’s comments section is certainly nothing short of heartwarming. Fans from all corners of the world have then come together to express their enthusiasm for her upcoming music.

Comments like “Can’t wait!” from fans and “yesssss” from her fellow American Idol alum HunterGirl, flooded in. It’s evident that Marlene’s music has created a dedicated following eagerly awaiting her next musical release.

The comments section also serves as a reminder of the profound connection between musicians and their audience. As Marlene’s fans eagerly await the release of Flashes, the excitement and support they’ve shown in the comments section only foreshadow the incredible reception her new music is likely to receive.
