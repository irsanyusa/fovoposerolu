Martha Stewart, 82, Stuns in Silver Dress With a Daring Thigh-High Slit

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Friday, July 19, 2024

Martha Stewart, at 82, defies age stereotypes, dazzling in a daring silver dress by Andrew Yu, as captured in a recent Instagram post.

“Fun to dress up every now and then,” Stewart captioned the photo, exuding confidence. The renowned home design expert complemented her look with silver heels and elegant shoulder-dusting earrings.

Enthusiastic responses flooded in from her followers, praising her timeless elegance and vivacity. “Martha still out here slaying!! You are gorgeous!! The epitome of style and grace mixed with fire and sass!” one admirer commented. While others added, “She grabs life by the tail and keeps reinventing herself and moving forward. I admire her.”

Stewart confidently displays her timeless beauty, boldly stating in an interview that her renowned “thirst traps” will continue to grace her feed. “People like pretty pictures,” she emphasized, not minding the critics who think she posts these photos to cover up her age.

“I don’t think about that,” the former TV host clarified. “I don’t think about age.”

In a groundbreaking moment, Stewart became the oldest cover model in Sports Illustrated Swimsuit history, radiating in a white one-piece swimsuit and a striking orange cover-up.
