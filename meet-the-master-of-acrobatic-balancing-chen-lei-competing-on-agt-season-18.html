Meet The Master of Acrobatic Balancing, Chen Lei, Competing on 'AGT' Season 18

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, July 20, 2024

TALENTRECAP.COM Talent America's Got Talent

Photo by: Trae Patton/NBC

Reading Time: 2 minutes

Meet Chen Lei, a 25-year-old acrobat from China who has captivated audiences worldwide with his awe-inspiring talent. Moreover, his decision to compete in America’s Got Talent season 18 exemplifies his spirit and eagerness to test his skills.

Who is Chen Lei?

Chen has practiced the art of acrobatics and balancing since he was a child, and has performed in a number of shows and competitions, including the Cirque du Soleil. Having dedicated his life to acrobatics since childhood, Chen relocated to the United States in 2016 to chase his dreams of becoming a professional performer. With his boundless talent and unwavering dedication, Chen’s future shines brightly.

Chen’s audition on season 18 of AGT left both judges and viewers astounded. Demonstrating extraordinary balance and control, he performed a gravity-defying routine on stilts, effortlessly maintaining a split pose.

The judges were on the edge of their seats at Chen’s skills, leading them to rise for a standing ovation. Howie Mandel expressed his astonishment, Simon Cowell acknowledged Chen as a genuine artist, while Sofía Vergara confessed to being profoundly impressed by his performance.

Chen’s audition not only left a lasting impression on the judges but also inspired countless individuals worldwide. Messages of admiration and motivation poured in from people all around the globe, deeply moved by Chen’s inspiring journey. Through his platform on AGT, Chen aims to ignite the flame of ambition in others and convey the message that with determination, “anything is possible.”

He Competed on China’s Got Talent

Chen also competed in China’s Got Talent in 2019, his acts include pretending to be a secret agent dodging lasers and then proceeding to play music while doing handstands. He received a standing ovation from the judges and the audience.

Chen’s act was a visually stunning display of balance, skill, and athleticism. He performed a series of acrobatic feats, including walking on a tightrope, balancing on his head, and doing backflips. He also played the violin while doing handstands, which was a truly impressive feat.

Chen’s performance on China’s Got Talent was a major accomplishment, and it helped to solidify his status as one of the world’s top acrobats. Audiences anticipate being mesmerized by his breathtaking performances on AGT, as he remains a top-tier act on the world stage.
