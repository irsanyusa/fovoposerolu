When Lagos stood still for Cubana Lagos grand opening

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Wednesday, July 31, 2024

Nigeria's center of excellence, Lagos, was practically shut down on that day when the Cubana Lagos officially opened its world-class facility on the ever busy Adeola Odeku Street in Victoria Island, for its customers.

The brainchild of Nigeria's entrepreneur of note, Obinna Oyiegbu and his luxury business consultant Nigerian-Lebanese corporate colossus, Joe Nazzal, is no doubt, a winning combination.

ADVERTISEMENT

Describing the facility which is said to be one of its kind in these parts, Oyiegbu said the atmosphere at the Cubana Lagos can best be described as cozy and the settings simply classical while the architectural masterpiece is out of this world.

"The major attraction is the Club Pablo which is a complete entertainment place that has a lounge, club, restaurant, boutique and valet parking", he said.

At the grand opening, top Nigerian celebrities like Toke Makinwa, E-money, Kcee, Lynxx, Frank Okamigbo, and others were not left out of the historic moment as everyone turned out resplendently.

A statement from the media team of the new lounge reads:

"It was a carnival-like atmosphere as Nigeria's circle of rich kids honored Club Pablo with their presence and resources too.

The grand entry of Club Pablo is not really a surprise - the gentlemen behind the club are some of Nigeria's brilliant minds.

Club Pablo is not just the regular Nigerian clubs with same routine services. At Cubana Lagos, there is something for everyone - perhaps, this is one of the few Nigerian clubs with an automated champagne service.

Not only that, this luxury club can comfortably accommodate 1000 people and here at Club Pablo, you will find a top of the range hi-tech lighting.

Also on offer at Cubana Lagos is the rooftop lounge called The Grind, which is a sky-view, all glass restaurant and bar purposefully built to create a 'wow' effect with some of the world's and managed by top-class chefs of international repute.

Cubana Lagos has a nightclub called Club Pablo and the grind, an all glassed restaurant and loin then and open bar Cuban hit n grill.

Another attraction at Cubana Lagos is the stylishly designed outdoor lounge known as Cubana Hut 'n' Grill that provides a natural feel Grill house and a fish pond for its 'point n kill' pepper soup special inside its bush bar.

Cubana Hut  n Grill is the traditional bush bar of the Cubana group brand, providing the best of African cuisine cutting across all national dishes," the statement concludes.
