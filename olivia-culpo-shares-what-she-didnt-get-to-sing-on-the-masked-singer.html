This week on The Masked Singer, UFO was unmasked as Miss USA and Miss Universe winner Olivia Culpo. In an interview after her unmasking, the pageant queen shared the artists she would have liked to cover on the show if she got the chance.

Olivia Culpo (UFO) Talks About The Masked Singer

Culpo told People that she started playing the cello at age 6, and her family has always loved singing together. Her brother, who’s a pianist, also encourages her to sing with him on TikTok. When deciding to do The Masked Singer, Culpo said she wanted to get “outside of my comfort zone.”

However, not even her brother knew she was competing on the show, as she shared that her family “cannot keep a secret.” Culpo said she didn’t expect to do so well in the competition, adding that she was “really excited to make it as far as I did.”

Culpo also said there were “so many” songs she didn’t get to sing, explaining, “I really wanted to sing something Christina Aguilera. That didn’t end up happening because I got the boot. And then I really wanted to sing Shania Twain, but I was not there for their Country Night. That was sad because just love country music. And I really love Shania Twain.”

Culpo Is Currently Planning Her Wedding

Last month, Culpo got engaged to NFL player Christian McCaffrey after around four years of dating. She told People that she’s currently in the process of planning their wedding, saying, “That’s a lot of work. Really fun, though.”

On top of that, Culpo shared that she’s starring in an upcoming movie called Clawfoot, but she’s not sure when it will be released. The star also teased, “And there are a few secret projects that I can’t talk about yet, but I’ll be announcing them.”

With UFO eliminated, there are only three contestants left going into the Semifinals of The Masked Singer Season 9 — California Roll, Macaw, and Medusa. Who will be crowned the winner?
