Whatever happened to Sofie Dossi from America’s Got Talent? This talented young star auditioned for the show on season 11, where she showed off her skills as a contortionist. She placed in the top 10 that year before returning for America’s Got Talent: The Champions. The self-taught contortionist has been making waves ever since.

Sofie has become insanely popular since her time on the show. Today, the performer has a whopping 4.1 million followers on Instagram and 10.2 million followers on TikTok. Sofie also posts regularly on her successful YouTube channel for her 6.32 million subscribers.

Sofie Dossi on YouTube

Sofie’s YouTube channel features the star as she takes on a number of dares. In one challenge, Sofie literally jumped off a bridge into a body of water. The video alone earned over 11 million views. Part two to the video performed even better, with a whopping 14 million. The channel also features unique 24 hour challenges such as: “Spending 24 Hours in the Air,” and “Surviving 24 Hours in the Ocean Bent in Half.”

Do Not Try This at Home!

Sofie also sprinkles in a number of popular dance videos on her channel. Her original dance music video to Marshmello and Ann-Marie’s “Friends” raked in over 127 million views on her channel. Other popular dance videos include her interpretation of Ariana Grande’s “7 Rings” and “My Boy” by Billie Eilish.

Sofie also thrives on TikTok, featuring a page full of viral content. In this space, she shows off her flexibility, comedy, and dancing in the short-form videos.

Sofie on America’s Got Talent

The contortionist and hand balancer auditioned for America’s Got Talent on season 11. Just 14-years-old at the time, her tricks consisted of bending her knees over her head and balancing on one hand. She even used her free hand to take a bite out of an apple for some extra flare.

Finally, while in a handstand position, she shot a bow and arrow with her feet, hitting a target at a distance. In the voting process, all three judges gave a “yes,” sending her through to the next round.

In the Judge Cuts, Sofie hung on an elevated hoop as she shot a flaming arrow from a bow with her feet. At this stage, she performed well enough to earn the Golden Buzzer from the guest judge, sending her straight to the Quarterfinals. Ultimately, she made it to the finale where she performed a series of aerial tricks. Unfortunately, she did not receive enough votes to be included in the top five, eliminating her from the competition.

However, that was not the last we would see from Sofie on the AGT stage. The performer returned for The Champions and made it to the preliminary stage. Just a few months ago, Sofie returned once again to pay a visit to the set. She documented the experience on her YouTube channel as she had fun with the judges and Terry Crews. In a cute segment, she even challenged the host to a strength contest.
