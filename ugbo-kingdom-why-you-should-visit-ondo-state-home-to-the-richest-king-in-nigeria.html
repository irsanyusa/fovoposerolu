Why you should visit Ondo state, home to the richest king in Nigeria

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Saturday, July 27, 2024

Ondo, often regarded as "The Sunshine State", was created on February 3, 1976. Its state capital is the popular Akure, while there are some other popular local governments such as Akoko, Okitipupa, Ondo town and Owo.

Ondo is home to the "biggest palace in Africa" as well as the richest king in Nigeria. Oba Obateru Akinrutan is the Olugbo (traditional King) of Ugbo land, a small oil-rich community in Ilaje local goverment area. He is worth over $300 million gotten from his businesses in oil. Ugbo Kingdom is a fisherman town along the coastline of the South-Western region of the country, which explains the oil deposits.

1. Idanre Hills

Idanre Hills is a beautiful landscape located in the quiet town of Idanre local government is Idanre hills which paint the landscape with its beauty. It is a cultural, economical and lifestyle lodestone to its community. You are welcome to climb if you dare.

2. Owo Museum of Artifacts

This was built in 1968 to accommodate the antiquities which were formerly in the Olowo Palace. It is notable as the site of significant archaeological and ethnographic material discovered in Owo area.

3. Cave of Ashes

ADVERTISEMENT

The cave played a significant role in the archaeological history of West Africa. Discovered in 1922 during a hunting expedition, it is notable as one of the key sources to the information about the history of West Africa

4. Ebomi Lake

According to the inhabitants of Ipesi Akoko, the lake has supernatural powers for protecting the villagers during the times of war. It is believed to have unseen soldiers that fight battles for the Ipesi people.

5. Igbo Olodumare

Igbo Olodumare is famed to be home to demons, spirits and other supernatural beings. Adventurers and tourists come from far and near to confirm this myth.

6. Filmhouse cinema, Akure

Filmhouse offers locals and tourists the chance to escape the quaint town with popcorn and the latest Box Office movie. It has all you need in abundance to give you the best viewing pleasure.

7. Smokin Hills Golf Resort

This resort was built in 2012 and is the perfect destination for anyone searching for an international standard golf course. It blends the modernity of a golf course with the tranquility of the ancient town.
